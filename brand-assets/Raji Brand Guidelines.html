clear space

give the mark room.

Clear space around the wordmark equals the height of the lowercase 'a' — what we call 1x. Nothing crosses this margin: not headlines, photos, edges of containers, or other logos.

1×
clear space on all sides — non-negotiable for prints, hero placements
88px
minimum digital size for the primary lockup
12mm
minimum print size — never smaller, never. The honey dot disappears.

voice & tone

like a friend who writes well.

Lowercase by default. Sentence case for everything except product names. Em-dashes, not semicolons. One idea per sentence. Real specifics over corporate adjectives. Never "elevate," never "robust," never "synergy."
specific
unhurried
warm
precise
never · "salesy"
never · jargon
caption · launch post

finally — three months of testing, eleven shade tweaks, one quiet drop. lipgloss no. 1 is here. tell me your favourite name in the comments & i'll send you a free one.

We are thrilled to announce the launch of our revolutionary new lipgloss! Crafted with passion, designed for the modern woman. Don't miss out — limited stock available!

imagery

soft light, real hands.

Warm daylight. Linen, ceramic, paper, hand-knit. Real hands holding real objects. No corporate stock, no fluorescent lighting, no perfect grids of products. Subjects: studios mid-work, half-finished cups of chai, handwritten notes, packaging in golden hour.

  • ·Crop close. Hands and small objects beat full-room shots.
  • ·Use the curved-bottom mask for portraits (top circle, bottom square corners).
  • ·Photos always sit on cream — never on white, never on the honey accent.
  • ·One tinted overlay only when matching the cocoa duotone for moody sections.
